Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website forms, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, business name, and project details. If you use our Client Hub, we also store your login credentials securely through our authentication provider (Supabase).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide website design services, manage your project through the Client Hub, and communicate updates about your project. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website: Formspree (form submissions), Supabase (authentication and data storage), Vercel (website hosting), and Google Fonts (typography). Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Client Hub data is secured with row-level security policies, and all communications are encrypted in transit via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

Our website uses session storage for functional purposes (such as remembering loader animation state). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at luminarch2026@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at luminarch2026@gmail.com.
